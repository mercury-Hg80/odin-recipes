Lasagna Recipe

Description

When John Chandler submitted this lasagna recipe to Allrecipes more than 20 years ago he had no idea how successful it would become. One of our top performing recipes of all time, World's Best Lasagna racks up more than seven million views per year and has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes for two decades.

Unfortunately, John passed away at 53 years old. The process is time consuming but the results are well worth the effort.

Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
