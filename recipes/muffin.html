Muffin Recipe

Description

These basic but delicious muffins can be made as is, or you may add ingredients to make them in a specific style

Ingredients
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners.
  3. Stir flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar together in large bowl; make well in center.
  4. Beat egg with fork in small bowl or 2-cup measuring cup; whisk in milk and oil. Pour egg mix at once into flour mixture; mix quickly and lightly with a fork until I just moistened
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the muffin top springs back when pressed, 25 minutes or so of baking time required.
