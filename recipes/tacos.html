Meat tacos

Taco meat recipe

Description

This taco meat recipe makes the best filling for tacos. You have to buy everything else, of course. Make a big batch and use it for multiple different meals: add to bean burritos or mix with a can of beans (kidney, red, or pinto) and use for Frito pie, Navajo tacos, or taco salad.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • A single 8oz can of tomato sauce
Steps
  1. Heat large skillet, medium high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in hot skillet until browned and crumbly for 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin. Pour tomato sauce over beef, stir to coat, simmer until slightly thickened. Can take about 5 minutes.
